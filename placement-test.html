Where Should You Start?
Why this test exists: the 12-week EV Diagnosis & Repair program (Weeks 1-8 theory/simulator + Weeks 9-12 real-vehicle OJT) assumes you already have basic ICE-technician-level skills. This 20-question test checks whether that's true for you yet.

What happens with your result: a high score means you can start directly at Week 1 of the 12-week program. A lower score means you should complete the 4-week / 20-session ICE Fundamentals Preliminary track first — that's not a failure, it's the track doing its job correctly.
SECTION 1 — SYSTEMS & SAFETY
Vehicle systems, shop safety, documentation, Ohm's Law
SECTION 2 — DIAGNOSTIC REASONING
The 4-step pattern, multimeter use, DTC basics
SECTION 3 — ELECTRICAL SYSTEMS
12V battery/charging, wiring diagrams, sensor-actuator-ECU
SECTION 4 — PROFESSIONAL READINESS
Documentation, communication, safety culture
