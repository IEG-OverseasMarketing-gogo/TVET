Track A — Korea Polytechnics Priority 1
Phase 1 · Immediate – 1 month

Connect & assess status

  • Reach out via IEG's existing contact at Polytechnics' overseas partnerships team
  • Confirm progress on the Mongolia International TVET Center
  • Check whether departments and equipment specs are already under discussion
Goal — Get to the table before specifications are finalized
Phase 2 · 1 – 6 months

Co-design equipment & curriculum

  • Propose training equipment configurations (electrical, PLC, smart manufacturing, EV) by department
  • Localize Korean-style TVET curriculum and develop Mongolian-language materials
  • Explore ties with local partner institutions such as Universal Polytechnic College
Goal — Build IEG's 6030-line standard into the center's specifications
Phase 3 · 6 months – 2 years

Formal supply & pilot operation

  • Sign equipment supply agreement; run a pilot tied to the "1+2 program"
  • Connect to budget and administrative support under the Education Ministry TVET MOU
  • Use the Mongolia center as a springboard into Central Asia
Goal — Secure standing as an ongoing education partner, not a one-off vendor
Track B — Dr. Gana's Network Priority 2
Phase 1 · Short-term (1–3 months)

Connect & identify needs

  • Formally approach Dr. Gana under the "USD 1B trade target" framing
  • Learn the current state of Mongolia's tourism & hospitality workforce training
  • Confirm demand for smart training equipment
Goal — Reduce entry risk through a trusted local relationship
Phase 2 · Mid-term (4–12 months)

Build DX training infrastructure

  • Co-develop vocational training programs for service & hospitality
  • Propose a university pilot via the AEN / CHRIE Asia-Pacific network
  • Join seminars and demos with Mongolian government tourism bodies
Goal — Provide AI/digital training to raise service quality
Phase 3 · Long-term (1–3 years)

Expand into supply-chain & industrial solutions

  • Propose critical-minerals-linked infrastructure & logistics systems (DX Labeler)
  • Pursue feasibility studies tied to KOICA IBS / EDCF funding
  • Formalize MOUs with Mongolian partner institutions
Goal — Large-scale system exports linked to government projects
